# Charlotte Reading 1 Discussion

Parts Man 1, Stage Directions

---
### Observations
- Two brothers are lost
- Joyce is helping her mom, Hattie is being grumpy
- Two stories are happening at the same time
- Both Mothers lose Husbands
- The photo shatters
- Marjean has difficulty with honesty
- Musical
- Joyce is working very had to help her mom
- The projector is used as a device in the storytelling
- Marjean seems to be giving into Joyce's pushing
- Hattie, and Marjean present this "bullet proof" type self image to others
- John also presents a different type of self image

---
### Warm Fuzzies
- Don greets his father with a smile
- Love the parallels made between modern day and pioneer day\
- Dead family members are dressed in white
- Liked the call backs to the journal scene 
- Merged really nicely
- Broke Andrew cynicism
- Liked children
- All characters were powerful
- Worked with the complexity of faith
- Liked tackling difficult concepts
- I really liked this work. I felt moved at many moments.

### Questions

- What audience do you have this in mind for?
- How do you inspire and realistically show hope and faith in times of trial?
- What is the purpose of this piece?
- Why is Joyce so thoughtful and precise when interacting with her mother? is she not frustrated or hurt by what happened too?
